About Us

Imprinting layers of skills, talents, & experiences

The Challenge

Today, in Kenya, several young people lack aspiration in STEM fields, both in academia and industry. They don't see opportunity for their futures in these fields which consequently leads to impeded socio-economic development and further stigmatisation of STEM careers.

Our Vision

We are on a journey to educate, empower and incubate young Scientists, Technologists, Engineers and Mathematicians (STEMists) and create leaders of tomorrow through pioneering collaborative engagement.

Our Mission

With a special focus on gender equality, we aspire to excite, inspire and generate opportunities to increase awareness and reduce the stigma surrounding STEM pathways.
